KOMAROW_ ART DESIGN Studio

KOMAROW ART DESIGN Studio is a Warsaw-based design studio that combines the rootedness of a local architecture practice with the breadth of interests of a global design firm.



What we do
Apartments, houses, cinemas, hotels, restaurants, offices, cafes, clubs, stores, shopping centres, and clinics all have interiors designed by us. Shopping and amusement centres, corporate centres, residential complexes, swimming pools, and exposition halls are all examples of architectural design. Designing the subject, the setting, and the navigation. Identity and branding are two things that we work on.

Why us
We carry out the entire design process, beginning with the concept and concluding with the product's full implementation. As a result, the project comes near to perfection, having been meticulously planned down to the last detail. We place a high value on close customer collaboration. We work for the customer, so we consider all of their needs and desires and bring them all together in a harmonious manner. We develop a distinct, unique style as well as a unique ideology.
